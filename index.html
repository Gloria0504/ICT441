    School profile

    Pigeon college is a public university located in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. The University has a strong reputation in the areas of managing project risk, managing and communicating ICT solutions, collecting data to determine business needs, creating cybersecurity standards for organizations, and interacting with customers on a business level. It offers relevant courses including BSBPMG536, ICTSAD604, ICTICT523, ICTCYS601 and ICTICT608 to help students acquire professional skills in the fields of business and technology.
