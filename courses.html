Courses

BSBPMG536
Manage project risk

BSBPMG536 is a course focused on project risk management. It provides students with the fundamental theoretical and practical skills of project risk management, including knowledge of risk identification, assessment, response and monitoring. The course also covers how to develop a risk management plan to ensure the smooth running of the project.

ICTSAD604
Manage and communicate ICT solutions

Ictsad 604 is a course on managing and communicating ICT solutions. The programme focuses on developing students' technical and management skills to be able to plan, implement and manage ICT projects effectively. Students will learn how to identify and solve technical problems and communicate effectively with team members and customers.

ICTICT523
Gather data to identify business requirements

ICTICT523 is a course that focuses on collecting data to determine business needs. By studying this course, students will understand how to collect and analyze data to determine the needs and goals of an organization. Students will learn to use a variety of tools and techniques to collect and collate data and use this data to develop effective business strategies for organizations.

ICTCYS601
Create cyber security standards for organisations

ICTCYS601 is a course on creating organizational cybersecurity standards. The course focuses on developing students' cybersecurity skills to ensure the security of an organization's network systems and data. Students will learn how to develop and implement a cybersecurity strategy to protect against various cyber threats and attacks.

ICTICT608
Interact with clients on a business level

ICTICT608 is a course on business-level interaction with customers. The programme focuses on developing students' communication skills and business knowledge in order to be able to interact and collaborate effectively with clients. Students will learn how to understand customer needs, develop business plans and solutions, and build long-term relationships.
